Website Gallery
In the 2020-2021 Fall term in the Department of Industrial Design, the ID 413 Interactive Prototyping for Designers course provided students with the knowledge to design and code a website without any third-party applications or web frameworks.

In this landing page, the exhibition presents the outcomes of the semester in the form of websites. Each student designed and published his/her website, which reflects their work with their unique style.
